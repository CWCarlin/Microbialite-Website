Microbialite Database

The Microbialites database houses information regarding multiple rock formations with microbialites, images of these formations, and samples collected from southern Nevada. With this database, we want researchers and other users to access and read this information through a website, and we also want them to be able to add information regarding any new findings of microbialites from the region. Customer support employees will be able to log technical problems and other employees can administer microbialites information.
